Plant Library

Indoor Plants

  • Snake Plant

    Snake plants are easy to care for and require little water. They prefer bright, indirect light.

  • Pothos

    Pothos are low-maintenance plants that can tolerate a wide range of conditions. They prefer bright, indirect light.

  • ZZ Plant

    ZZ plants are drought-tolerant and can survive in low light conditions. They prefer bright, indirect light.

  • Rubber Plant

    Rubber plants are known for their large, glossy leaves. They thrive in bright, indirect light.

  • Spider Plant

    Spider plants are easy to grow and are great for purifying indoor air. They prefer bright, indirect light.

  • Aloe Vera

    Aloe vera is a succulent with many health benefits. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Outdoor Plants

  • Lavender

    Lavender is known for its fragrance and is used in aromatherapy. It requires full sun and well-drained soil.

  • Rosemary

    Rosemary is a fragrant herb used in cooking and has medicinal properties. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

  • Sunflower

    Sunflowers are tall, cheerful flowers that attract pollinators. They need full sun and well-drained soil.

  • Hibiscus

    Hibiscus flowers come in various colors and are popular in gardens. They require full sun and regular watering.

  • Geranium

    Geraniums are colorful flowers often used in containers and hanging baskets. They prefer full sun and well-drained soil.

  • Marigold

    Marigolds are easy-to-grow flowers that come in vibrant colors. They require full sun and well-drained soil.
